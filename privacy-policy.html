Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Color Sort ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Color Sort is a puzzle game that does not require account registration. We collect limited information to improve your experience and serve advertisements:

  • Gameplay data: Level progress, scores, and streak information stored locally on your device via PlayerPrefs.
  • Advertising data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized ads.
  • Analytics: General app usage statistics may be collected anonymously to improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — advertising platform. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — core Android services. Google Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Game progress (level, score, streak) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not maintain any external database of user information.

Children's Privacy

Color Sort is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to play.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads. AdMob uses cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
